Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. How does online personal training work?

Your coach will guide your workouts according to your fitness goals through video calling. They will demonstrate the exercises and then assess your movements and form to ensure proper training.

Q2. What can I expect during a nutrition counseling session?

You will consult a qualified nutrition expert who will evaluate your medical history, current eating habits, diet preferences, and health goals. They will design a personalized nutrition plan that aligns with your objectives.

Q3. Are your trainers and nutrition experts certified?

Yes, all our coaches and nutritionists are certified professionals with the necessary qualifications and experience to provide you with top-notch guidance and support.

Q4. Can I participate if I’m a beginner with no prior fitness or nutrition knowledge?

Absolutely! Our services are designed to accommodate individuals of all fitness levels, including beginners. Whether you are new to exercise or nutrition, our experts will create a program tailored to your specific needs and gradually progress as you gain confidence and experience.

Q5. What equipment do I need for online personal training sessions?

The equipment requirements will vary depending on your current fitness status. Some exercises may require basic equipment like dumbbells, resistance bands, or a stability ball. If you don't have access to equipment or you are a beginner, our trainers can modify your program accordingly.

HQ6. I'm so busy with work and family commitments. Can this program fit into my hectic schedule?

Absolutely! One of the benefits of online training is its flexibility. We understand that life can get busy, so our program provides flexibility to fit workouts even in the busiest schedules, ensuring you can stay on track without feeling overwhelmed.

HQ7. I've tried various diets and exercise programs before and failed. How is this different?

We understand that every individual is unique, and what works for one person may not work for another. Our personalized approach takes into account your specific goals, preferences, and challenges. By tailoring the program to your needs, we aim to create a sustainable and enjoyable fitness and nutrition journey that sets you up for long-term success.

Q8. I don't have access to fancy gym equipment. Can I still benefit from online training?

Definitely! Our trainers are skilled at creating effective workouts with minimal or no equipment. Whether you have a fully-equipped home gym or just a small space with a yoga mat, we'll design a program that maximizes your resources.

Q9. I have a medical condition/injury. Can I still participate in online training?

It's crucial to prioritize your health and safety. When you sign up, you'll have an opportunity to share any medical conditions or injuries with our experts. They will guide you with the exercises that take these factors into account, ensuring you can still make progress while staying within your limits.

Q10. Can I switch trainers or nutrition experts if needed?

If you feel the need to switch trainers or nutrition experts for any reason, please contact our support team, and we will accommodate your request promptly.

Q11. How do I get started?

To get started, simply visit our website and sign up for the Free 3-day trial. Once you're registered, we'll guide you through the onboarding process and pair you with our experts to begin your journey to a healthier lifestyle.
